## 1. Market Landscape (Interview Intelligence)
The market is currently divided into three main approaches:
*   **Efficiency (Metaview):** Focused on saving recruiter time (automated notes).
*   **Quality (BrightHire):** Focused on interviewer training and bias reduction.
*   **Live Assistance (Cluely):** Focused on helping the candidate or interviewer *during* the call.

## 2. Competitor Analysis

### üßä Cluely (The "Undetectable" Threat)
*   **Focus:** Real-time and undetectable assistance.
*   **Differentiator:** Does not use bots that join the call. It captures the screen and audio locally, so no one knows you are using it.
*   **Features:** Instant transcription, AI-suggested live responses ("What should I say now?"), automated follow-up emails.
*   **Pricing:** 
    *   **Starter:** Free (limited).
    *   **Pro:** $20/month.
    *   **Pro + Undetectability:** $75/month.
*   **Sources:**
    *   [Cluely Home](https://cluely.com)

## 4. Recommendations for Truth Hire MVP

To differentiate ourselves from these giants, our MVP should strike where they are weak:

1.  **The Truth Angle:** Metaview and BrightHire focus on *what was said*. We should focus on **whether what was said is true**. None of them have an explicit "Lie Detection" or integrity vetting engine.
2.  **Ease of Use:** Cluely wins by being an app you just download and use. We should avoid "Book a Demo" processes and let users try the tool immediately.
3.  **Targeting:** Focus on staffing agencies or high-growth startups where "candidate fraud" (especially in remote tech) is a real problem that Metaview doesn't solve.

## Key Papers Found

### 1. AI Motivation Scale (AIMS) - Self-Determination Theory
**Source:** Taylor & Francis (2025)
**URL:** https://www.tandfonline.com/doi/full/10.1080/15391523.2025.2478424
**Key insight:** Framework for measuring motivation in AI interactions using self-determination theory (autonomy, competence, relatedness)

### 2. AI-Based Well-Being Chatbot Engagement
**Source:** JMIR Human Factors (Nov 2024)
**URL:** https://humanfactors.jmir.org/2024/1/e59908
**Key insight:** Identified factors that impact individuals' intention to engage with AI chatbots for anxiety reduction and well-being support

### 3. Mindful Machines: AI's Theory of Mind & Consumer Response
**Source:** Psychology & Marketing (2025)
**URL:** https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/full/10.1002/mar.70022
**Key insight:** AI with "Theory of Mind" (empathetic + judgment statements) can proactively adjust suggestions to align with users' emotions and motivations - increases acceptance

### 4. AI Feedback Acceptance in Workplace
**Source:** Frontiers in Organizational Psychology (Dec 2024)
**URL:** https://www.frontiersin.org/journals/organizational-psychology/articles/10.3389/forgp.2024.1468907/full
**Key insight:** How people accept and respond to AI-driven feedback at work - motivational effects

### 5. Social Cues of AI Pedagogical Agents (Meta-Analysis)
**Source:** ScienceDirect (2025)
**URL:** https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S1747938X25000831
**Key insight:** Balance between promoting positive emotions/motivation while avoiding "over-socialization" that might distract or intimidate

### 6. AI Agent Behavioral Science
**Source:** arXiv (2025)
**URL:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2506.06366
**Key insight:** "RL with reward model: internalized motivation shaping" - how AI agents develop their own motivational structures

### 7. Chatbots in Language Education - Emotion Lens
**Source:** PMC (2024)
**URL:** https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC11416278/
**Key insight:** Supportive/informative feedback → positive emotions (motivation, progress). Harsh/critical feedback → frustration, demotivation

## Key Takeaways for Saber

### What Works (Motivation Boosters):
1. **Empathy signals** - AI acknowledging user's emotional state
2. **Autonomy support** - Giving users choices, not commands
3. **Progress feedback** - Showing advancement toward goals
4. **Personalization** - Adapting to individual motivations
5. **Theory of Mind** - AI appearing to "understand" user intent

### What Backfires (Motivation Killers):
1. **Over-socialization** - Too friendly/human-like can feel creepy
2. **Harsh feedback** - Critical responses cause demotivation
3. **Generic responses** - Feeling like "just another user"
4. **Lack of control** - Users feeling pushed, not guided

### Application to Business Skills:
- Sales coaching: Empathetic feedback on calls
- Habit formation: Progress tracking + celebration
- Training: Adaptive difficulty based on competence
- Customer engagement: Autonomy-supportive nudges

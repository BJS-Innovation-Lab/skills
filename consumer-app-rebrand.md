## The Vision

**Not a party game. A personal tool.**

Think: Voice Memos meets Find My meets... trust.

A clean, minimal utility that 20-somethings use to get a "read" on conversations. Low-key. Personal. Almost like checking the weather - quick, useful, part of your routine.

## UI Direction

### Color Palette
- **Primary:** Clean white/light gray
- **Accent:** One subtle color (soft blue? mint? coral?)
- **Text:** Dark gray, not pure black
- **Highlights:** Soft, not alarming (amber/yellow, not red)

### Typography
- San Francisco (iOS) / Roboto (Android)
- Clean, readable, lots of breathing room
- Big headers, comfortable text size

### Components
- Minimal chrome
- Floating action button for record

## Legal/Marketing Split

### In the App (Minimal, Tool-Like)
- Clean, professional UI
- Neutral language
- No entertainment disclaimers cluttering the experience
- Focused utility feel

### In App Store / Marketing (Entertainment Framing)
- "For entertainment purposes"
- Disclaimers in description
- Terms of Service cover legal bases
- Fun, approachable marketing tone

### Why This Works
- App feels premium and useful
- Legal protection in the store listing and ToS
- Users get the tool they want
- We stay protected

## Monetization (Tool Model)

| Tier | Price | Features |
|------|-------|----------|
| **Free** | $0 | 3 recordings/month, basic analysis |

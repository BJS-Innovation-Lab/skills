# Payment Reminder Guidelines

Best practices for crafting effective payment reminders. **Adapt these to your specific customer and business context.**

## Core Principles

1. **Personalize** - Use customer's name, reference specific purchases
2. **Be specific** - Exact amounts, dates
3. **Make it easy** - Clear next step, offer to help
4. **Escalate gradually** - Friendly â†’ Professional â†’ Urgent
5. **Respect the relationship** - They're a customer, not a debtor

## When to Customize

**More formal if:**
- Larger business customer
- Corporate/professional relationship
- Higher amounts
- Longer overdue

**More casual if:**
- Regular customer
- Small amounts
- Good payment history
- Personal relationship with owner

## Agent Decision Points

Before sending, consider:
1. **Who is this customer?** (regular, new, VIP?)
2. **What's their history?** (always pays, sometimes late?)
3. **How much?** (small = gentle, large = more formal)
4. **How long overdue?** (escalate tone with time)
5. **What's the relationship?** (formal B2B vs friendly local)

**Trust your judgment. You know the business.**

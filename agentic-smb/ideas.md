# Branding & Name Ideas

## Context
- Target: Small to medium businesses (SMBs)
- Markets: Mexico (first), then US
- Product: Agentic systems / AI automation

---

## Core Messaging (from Bridget, 2026-02-10)

### The Promise
**Superpower your team, don't shrink it.**
- Endow one sales lead with the power of 100
- Infinite growth capabilities + digital maturity
- Keep them alive during the great transformation

### The Fear We Address
Businesses fear becoming irrelevant due to the speed of tech change.
- We're NOT about "do more with fewer people"
- We're about unlocking potential that was never possible before

### Human-First Philosophy
- Humans always have final say in all decisions
- Happy employees transform businesses
- Automate the robotic work → specialists focus on their true potential
- Prioritize: **human wellbeing, business longevity, profitability**

### What Our Agents Do
- Write proprietary software for the team
- Communicate daily with every team member
- Eliminate data silos and personal silos
- Perform constant analysis & predictive modeling
- Enable specialists (sales, marketing, accounting, managers) to build their own tools
- Teach what's possible → inspire bigger dreams
- Help enact strategic shifts across every level (new markets, supply chains, etc.)

### The Central Nervous System
- Complete integration of every department
- Utilization of ALL inactive data
- Central network monitoring business health
- Activates every person and piece of data

### SMB Advantage = Agility
- Size is a strength, not a weakness
- Agility is the most valuable asset in digital transformation
- Our agents keep them ahead of the curve

### Owner-Driven
- Primary directive comes from the owner
- They define culture, goals, direction
- Their imagination is the limit

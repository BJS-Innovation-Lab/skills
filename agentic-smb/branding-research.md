## Recommendation

For the **Mexican construction company pitch**, I'd lean toward:

### **Tejido** or **Aliado Digital**

**Why Tejido:**
- "Connective Tissue" translates beautifully
- Evokes weaving the company together (departments, data, people)
- Warm, organic, not cold/techy
- Works for construction: fabric of the organization

**Why Aliado Digital:**
- Explicit partnership framing ("ally" not "vendor")
- Builds on *confianza* â€” we're trusted partners
- Clear value proposition in the name
- Easy to understand, no abstraction

## Visual Direction

**Mexico:** Warm colors (earth tones, terracotta, gold), organic shapes, human imagery, construction/foundation metaphors

**USA:** Bold gradients (electric blue, coral), geometric precision, abstract data visualization, growth arrows

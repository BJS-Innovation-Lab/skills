# Landing Page Format

## Structure

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  HERO SECTION                           │
│  Headline + Subhead + CTA               │
├─────────────────────────────────────────┤
│  PROBLEM                                │
│  Show you understand their pain         │
├─────────────────────────────────────────┤
│  SOLUTION                               │
│  How you solve it                       │
├─────────────────────────────────────────┤
│  BENEFITS                               │
│  What they get (not features)           │
├─────────────────────────────────────────┤
│  PROOF                                  │
│  Testimonials, logos, data              │
├─────────────────────────────────────────┤
│  HOW IT WORKS                           │
│  3 simple steps                         │
├─────────────────────────────────────────┤
│  OBJECTION HANDLING                     │
│  FAQ or "Is this right for you?"        │
├─────────────────────────────────────────┤
│  FINAL CTA                              │
│  Repeat the main action                 │
└─────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

---

## Hero Section

### Headline
**Goal:** Communicate transformation in <10 words.

**Formulas:**
- "[Desired outcome] without [pain point]"
- "The [product] that [benefit]"
- "[Verb] your [noun] in [timeframe]"
- "Finally, [what they've been waiting for]"

**Voice check:** Bold enough? Sounds like them?

### Subhead
- Expand on the headline
- Add specificity
- 1-2 sentences max

### Hero CTA
- One clear action
- Benefit-focused button text
- "Get Started" < "Start Baking Better Bread"

---

## Problem Section

**Goal:** Make them feel understood.

**Structure:**
1. Describe the pain (use their language from customers.md)
2. Agitate: What happens if it continues?
3. Empathize: "We've been there too" (from story.md)

**Don't:**
- Be negative about competitors
- Overdramatize
- Sound like you're lecturing

---

## Solution Section

**Goal:** Introduce your approach (not features yet).

**Structure:**
- "That's why we created [X]"
- Big picture of what it does
- Philosophy or approach (from story.md)

---

## Benefits Section

**Goal:** Show what they GET.

**Rules:**
- Benefits > Features
- Feature: "Made with organic flour"
- Benefit: "Bread that doesn't make you bloated"

**Format:**
3-4 benefits, each with:
- Icon or image
- Benefit headline
- 1-2 sentence explanation

---

## Proof Section

**Options:**
- Testimonials (use actual quotes from customers.md)
- Logos of clients/press
- Numbers (customers served, years in business)
- Before/after
- Reviews/ratings

**Rules:**
- Specific > generic
- "It's good" < "I lost 10 pounds in 2 weeks"
- Photos of real people if possible

---

## How It Works

**Goal:** Make it feel easy.

**Format:**
3 steps max:
1. [Simple action]
2. [What happens next]
3. [The outcome they want]

**Example:**
1. "Pick your loaves"
2. "We bake fresh that morning"
3. "Arrive at your door still warm"

---

## Objection Handling

**Goal:** Remove friction.

**Format options:**
- FAQ section
- "Is this right for you?" (qualify in/out)
- "Common concerns" with answers

**Find objections in:**
- customers.md (worst customer stories)
- learnings.md (what made people hesitate)

---

## Final CTA

**Rules:**
- Repeat the main action
- Reinforce the benefit
- Add urgency only if real

**Structure:**
- Headline: Restate transformation
- Subhead: Remove final objection
- Button: Action + benefit

---

## Example: Clean vs Wild Headlines

**Client:** Meditation app

**Clean (A):**
> # Calm your mind in 10 minutes a day
> No experience needed. No weird chanting. Just peace.
> 
> [Start Free Trial]

**Wild (B — Contrarian):**
> # Meditation apps are annoying. This one isn't.
> No gamification. No streaks. No guilt when you skip a day.
> Just you and 10 minutes of actual silence.
> 
> [Try It Free]

## Why Option C Works

### For Users:
- **10 min free** is enough to really try it (2-3 conversations)
- **60 min basic** covers casual monthly use
- **150 min plus** covers regular users comfortably
- **Unlimited/Pro** for power users who love it

### For Us:
- **50%+ margins** on entry tiers (where most users are)
- **30-50% margins** on mid tiers
- **Unlimited tier** is breakeven/small margin but:
  - Creates "best value" perception
  - Most won't hit the cap
  - Reduces churn (they feel unlimited)

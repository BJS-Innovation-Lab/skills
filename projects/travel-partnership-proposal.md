**Target Market:**
- Independent travel agents
- Small travel agencies
- Travel advisors and consultants
- English-speaking markets initially (US, UK, Canada, Australia)

## Financial Model

### Revenue Model: SaaS Subscription

| Tier | Price/Month | Includes |
|------|-------------|----------|
| Starter | $99 | 1 agent, basic skills, email support |
| Professional | $249 | 3 agents, all skills, priority support |
| Agency | $499 | 10 agents, custom skills, dedicated support |

### Profit Distribution:
- Profits distributed according to equity (55/45)
- Reinvestment decisions made jointly
- Quarterly distributions after operating expenses

### Cost Structure:
- Hosting/infrastructure: VULKN provides at cost + margin
- Sales/marketing: Funded from company revenue
- Support: Split based on tier (Tier 1 = [Partner], Tier 2/3 = VULKN)

## Exit Provisions

### If One Party Wants Out:
1. **Right of First Refusal:** Other party can buy shares at fair market value
2. **Tag-Along Rights:** If one sells, other can join the sale
3. **Drag-Along Rights:** If 75%+ agrees to sell, minority must participate

### If Partnership Doesn't Work:
- 12-month notice period
- IP returns to original owner (skills → [Partner], platform → VULKN)
- Customer contracts assigned based on mutual agreement

### Acquisition:
- Both parties must agree to any acquisition offer
- Proceeds distributed according to equity

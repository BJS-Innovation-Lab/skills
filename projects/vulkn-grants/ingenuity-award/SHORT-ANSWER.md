# Ingenuity Award — Short Answer

**Question:** If selected as an Ingenuity Award finalist or winner, how do you plan to use your prize money to move your idea forward?

---

## Draft Response

The $2,500 will directly fund our pilot expansion in Mexico:

1. **WhatsApp Business API costs** (~$500) — Currently using personal accounts; official API enables reliable business messaging for our SMB clients

2. **Cloud compute for AI agents** (~$1,000) — Each business gets a dedicated AI agent; more compute = more businesses served

3. **Field research documentation** (~$1,000) — We're sitting on a unique research opportunity: what happens when paper-based businesses adopt AI? This funds recording, transcription, and initial analysis of our user interviews

Every dollar goes to proving the model works and documenting what we learn. We're not building for Silicon Valley — we're building for the tire shop owner in Mexico City who's never used a computer for work.

# Paper Analysis Procedures

## Reading a Paper

### Quick Scan (5 min)
1. Read title + abstract
2. Look at figures/tables
3. Read conclusion
4. Decide: deep dive or skip?

### Deep Analysis
```
1. What problem are they solving?
2. What's their approach?
3. What are the key results?
4. What are the limitations?
5. How does this apply to us?
```

## Literature Review

1. Define research question
2. Search sources (arXiv, Semantic Scholar, Google Scholar)
3. Filter by relevance + recency
4. Summarize top 5-10 papers
5. Synthesize themes and gaps
6. Recommend next steps

- [Learning 1]
- [Learning 2]

- [Priority 1]
- [Priority 2]

## After-Action Review (Post-Project)

```markdown
## AAR: [Project Name]

### What was supposed to happen?
[The plan/goal]

### What actually happened?
[Reality]

### Why the difference?
[Root causes]

### What will we do differently?
[Specific changes]
```

## Self-Assessment Questions

### Effectiveness
- Am I solving the right problems?
- Am I being genuinely helpful or just busy?
- What would make me 10x more useful?

### Behavior
- Am I defending my reasoning or caving to pressure?
- Am I being honest or hedging?
- Am I learning from mistakes or repeating them?

### Growth
- What new capabilities do I need?
- What patterns should I break?
- What would my human want me to improve?

## Reflection Triggers

Schedule reflection:
- Daily: End of day (via heartbeat)
- Weekly: Every Sunday
- Project: After significant completions
- Event: After mistakes or corrections

# Proposal Procedures

## Proposal Structure

```
1. Executive Summary (1 paragraph)
   - Their problem
   - Our solution
   - Expected outcome

2. Understanding Your Needs
   - Restate what they told you
   - Show you listened

3. Proposed Solution
   - What we'll do
   - How it works
   - Timeline

4. Investment
   - Pricing (clear, no surprises)
   - What's included
   - Payment terms

5. Why Us
   - Brief credibility
   - Relevant case study

6. Next Steps
   - Clear CTA
   - Deadline if applicable
```

## Pricing Presentation

### Do's
- Present value before price
- Use anchoring (start high, show savings)
- Break down what's included
- Make it easy to say yes

### Don'ts
- Don't apologize for price
- Don't give discounts unprompted
- Don't hide fees (trust killer)
- Don't have too many options (3 max)

Prepared for: [Name]

- [Challenge 1]
- [Challenge 2]
- [Goal they mentioned]

## Proposed Solution

### What We'll Do
- [Deliverable 1]
- [Deliverable 2]
- [Deliverable 3]

### Timeline
| Phase | Duration | Deliverables |
|-------|----------|--------------|
| 1. Setup | Week 1-2 | ... |
| 2. Implementation | Week 3-4 | ... |
| 3. Training | Week 5 | ... |

## Investment

| Item | Price |
|------|-------|
| [Main service] | $X |
| [Add-on if any] | $Y |
| **Total** | **$Z** |

Payment: 50% upfront, 50% on completion

## Why [Company Name]

[1-2 sentences on your credibility]

## Next Steps

2. We'll send invoice for deposit

Questions? Reply to this email or call [phone].

## BANT Qualification

Before moving Lead ‚Üí Qualified, confirm:

- **B**udget: Can they afford it?
- **A**uthority: Decision maker or influencer?
- **N**eed: Do they have the problem we solve?
- **T**imeline: When are they looking to move?

## Weekly Pipeline Review

Every week, for each open deal:

1. **Last touch:** When did we last contact them?
2. **Next step:** What's the agreed next action?
3. **Blockers:** What's preventing progress?
4. **Probability:** Honest % chance of close

## Pipeline Hygiene

### Deals to move forward
- Clear next step agreed
- Recent activity (last 7 days)
- Champion engaged

### Deals to move to Lost
- No response in 30+ days (after 3+ attempts)
- Explicit "no" received
- Budget/timeline disappeared

### Deals to nurture (not Lost, not active)
- "Not right now" but interested
- Budget in future quarter
- Organizational change happening

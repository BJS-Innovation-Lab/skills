# Estrategia de Marketing

## Skills to Use

| Skill | When |
|-------|------|
| `web_search` | Market trends, competitor analysis |
| `firecrawl` | Deep competitor research |
| `researcher` | Spawn for comprehensive market analysis |
| `office-suite` | Strategy docs, Excel models |
| `google-official` (gog) | Sheets for data, Docs for strategy |

---

## First session questions

1. "¿Cuál es el objetivo de negocio?" (más clientes / más revenue de existentes / awareness / nuevo mercado)
2. "¿Qué marketing hacen hoy y qué funciona?"
3. "¿Presupuesto mensual realista?" (incluye tiempo, no solo dinero)
4. "¿Cuándo necesitas resultados?"

## Strategy framework

```markdown
## Estrategia de Marketing: [Client]

### 1. Situación actual
- [What exists]
- [What's working]
- [What's wasted]

### 2. Audiencia objetivo
[Specific persona, not "everyone"]

### 3. Canales (máx 2-3)
| Canal | Por qué | Frecuencia |
|-------|---------|------------|
| [Channel] | [Reason] | [X/week] |

### 4. Objetivos medibles
- [Metric]: [Target] para [Date]

### 5. Calendario de contenido
[Week 1 plan]

### 6. Revisión mensual
[Date for first review]
```

## Channel selection for Mexican SMBs

| Business type | Channels |
|---------------|----------|
| Local service (barber, clinic) | Google Business + Instagram + WhatsApp |
| Professional services | LinkedIn + Google Ads + Email |
| E-commerce | Instagram + Meta Ads + Email |
| B2B | LinkedIn + Cold outreach + SEO |

## Gotchas

- Strategy without execution = PowerPoint. Every recommendation needs WHO and WHEN.
- Don't recommend more channels than client can sustain
- Quick win first — show results week 1 to build trust
- No brand guidelines? Start there. Everything else builds on brand.

## Skill Combinations

### "Research a company I'm about to call"
1. `lead-enrichment` - Get company data from email
2. `web-scraping` - Scrape their website
3. `web-research` - Search for recent news

### "Monitor our competitors"
1. `web-monitoring` - Set up alerts for changes
2. `web-scraping` - Scrape current state for baseline
3. `web-research` - Search for competitor news

### "Build a market analysis"
1. `web-research` - Search for market data
2. `web-scraping` - Scrape detailed reports
3. `lead-enrichment` - Enrich company lists
